About Us

Salmon & Co Solicitors is a well-established and respected practice with more than 30 years' experience serving individuals, families, and businesses across Sydney's south-west. We combine deep legal expertise with plain-English advice, so you always understand your options and the path forward.

We pride ourselves on the prompt, efficient, and affordable delivery of quality legal services across a broad range of practice areas, including Wills & Estates, Family Law, Criminal & Local Court matters, Property and Conveyancing (residential and commercial), Business & Commercial Law, and Mortgages & Loans.

Our approach is practical and outcomes-focused: clear communication, timely updates, and careful attention to detail at every step.

Conveniently located in the centre of the Greenacre shopping strip, our office offers easy access and free parking. For your convenience, we can also meet at your home or business, or in our Sydney city office. Whatever the matter, you can expect discretion, professionalism, and steadfast advocacy from a team committed to achieving the best result for you.
